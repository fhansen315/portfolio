## The Story

In 2024, I joined Ripple as an SDR. After my first month, I realized I was spending 95+ hours per campaign on repetitive manual tasks.

Instead of accepting this as normal, I taught myself Python, then TypeScript, then AI/ML. Over the next year, I built automation systems that:

- Reduced campaign prep from **95 hours to 30 minutes**
- Achieved **99.9% automation rate**
- Processed **127 leads in 2-3 minutes**
- Saved **50+ hours per week**

Now I'm looking to do this full-time—building automation systems for entire organizations, not just my own workflows.
