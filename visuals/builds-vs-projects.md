# Builds vs Projects

## Portfolio Philosophy

### Atomic Builds
**Definition**: Completed, standalone deliverables that have generated or are generating value.

**Characteristics**:
- Done - no remaining work required
- Value generated - measurable impact achieved
- Independent - works on its own
- Documented - fully captured

### Projects
**Definition**: Larger initiatives with significant progress but not fully complete.

**Characteristics**:
- In progress - work remaining
- Potential value - impact not yet realized
- Connected - depends on other systems
- Evolving - specifications may change
