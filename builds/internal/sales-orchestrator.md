# Sales Automation Orchestrator

**AI-Powered Revenue Acceleration** | Sales | 2025-2026

---

## Overview

Built master automation system orchestrating 4 AI workflows for sales operations, achieving 160x productivity improvement and 99.9% automation rate for lead processing.

---

## Problem

Manual sales processes were unsustainable:
- 30 min manual research per lead × 127 leads = 63.5 hours
- 10 min manual email drafting × 127 leads = 21 hours
- 5 min manual scoring × 127 leads = 10.5 hours
- **Total manual effort: 95+ hours per campaign cycle**

---

## Solution

Created Master Automation Orchestrator with 4 AI workflows:

### 1. Lead Enrichment & Prioritization
- LinkedIn decision-maker identification
- Trigger event detection from news
- Technology stack analysis
- Priority scoring (P0/P1/P2/P3)

### 2. Email Sequence Generation
- Personalized sequences per lead
- 3-7 touch sequences auto-generated
- Industry/geography customization
- 400-700 emails produced

### 3. Lead Scoring Engine
- Fit Score (40%): Assets, volume, geography
- Intent Score (30%): Trigger events, buying signals
- Engagement Score (30%): Activities, responses
- A/B/C/D grading system

### 4. Insights & Recommendations
- Pattern analysis across leads
- Top 10 opportunity identification
- Risk flagging
- Next-best-action recommendations
