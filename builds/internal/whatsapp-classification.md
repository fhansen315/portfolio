# WhatsApp Group Classification

**Enterprise Messaging Intelligence** | Automation | 2026

---

## Overview

Built automated classification system for 97 WhatsApp business groups, enabling intelligent monitoring and prioritization of partner communications.

---

## Problem

Managing 97+ WhatsApp groups manually was unsustainable:
- No visibility into message volume across groups
- Unable to prioritize partner communications
- Missing important mentions and keywords
- No analytics on engagement patterns

---

## Solution

Created comprehensive classification and monitoring system:

### Group Discovery & Classification
Auto-categorized 97 groups into:
- `partner-opportunity` (60) - Active deal groups
- `partner-general` (20) - General partner chats
- `internal` (8) - Internal team channels
- `event` (2) - Conference/event groups
- `master-automation` (1) - Bot UAT group
- `test` (1), `unclassified` (6)

### Label System Integration
- 13 WhatsApp Business labels synced
- Sales pipeline labels: Prospect → Cold → Warm → Opportunity → Customer
- Auto-mapping from categories to labels

### Analytics Framework
- Activity transformer: Message counts, hourly distribution
- Engagement transformer: Partner engagement scoring
- Response time transformer: SLA tracking
- Trend transformer: Week-over-week comparisons
