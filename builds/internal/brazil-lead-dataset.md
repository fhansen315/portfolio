**Enriched LATAM Sales Intelligence** | Sales | 2026

---

## Overview

Built comprehensive Brazil/LATAM lead database with 127 enriched, scored, and sequenced leads ready for immediate sales deployment.

---

## Problem

Sales team needed:
- Consolidated view of LATAM opportunities
- Prioritized lead ranking
- Pre-built outreach sequences
- Contact enrichment data

---

## Solution

Created end-to-end lead intelligence system:

### Data Consolidation
- Multiple source integration (Salesforce, manual lists)
- Deduplication and cleansing
- Contact information validation
- Account hierarchy mapping

### Enrichment
- Decision-maker identification
- Company size and revenue data
- Technology stack detection
- Recent news and trigger events

### Scoring & Prioritization
- **P0**: Immediate action required
- **P1**: High priority follow-up
- **P2**: Standard cultivation
- **P3**: Long-term nurture

### Sequence Assignment
- P0 leads: 7-touch aggressive sequence
- P1 leads: 4-touch standard sequence
- P2/P3 leads: 3-touch light sequence
