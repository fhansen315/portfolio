# EnergyYield Concept

**Tokenized Energy Yield Platform Design** | Research | 2026

---

## Overview

Designed first-of-its-kind platform applying Pendle Finance's yield tokenization model to Brazil's R$200B Free Energy Market, creating strategic investment proposal for Dcide LTDA & Justoken partnership.

---

## Problem

Brazil's energy market lacked yield derivatives:
- Capital locked in long-term PPAs with no liquidity
- Variable revenue exposure to spot price fluctuations
- No fixed-rate energy products available
- Limited hedging instruments domestically
- Institutional investors have no energy yield access

---

## Solution

Designed comprehensive platform concept:

### Market Opportunity
- Brazil Free Energy Market: 37.2% of national consumption
- Growing 26% YoY with 13,800+ H1 2025 migrations
- Full market liberalization expected by 2027
- Total addressable market: ~R$200B annually

### Platform Architecture
- **Energy PT** (Principal Token): Fixed yield claim on energy assets
- **Energy YT** (Yield Token): All future revenue rights
- Dcide oracle integration for pricing
- Justoken rails for tokenization

### Product Suite
- Energy PT Markets: CDI+2-5% fixed yield
- Energy YT Markets: Leveraged yield exposure
- PPA Tokenization: Fractional ownership
- I-REC Yield Splits: Renewable certificate trading

### Strategic Partnerships
- **Dcide LTDA**: Brazil's energy price oracle since 2012
- **Justoken**: Top-3 global tokenized commodity issuer
